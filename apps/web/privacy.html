Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Baby Names ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and optional last name you provide when creating an account.
  • Usage data: Baby name preferences (swipe decisions, liked names, custom lists) you create in the app.
  • Device data: Anonymous analytics events (e.g., screen views) to improve the app. No personally identifying information is included.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the app experience.
  • To sync your preferences across devices when you are signed in.
  • To send transactional emails (email verification, password reset) — never marketing without your consent.
  • To improve the app via aggregated, anonymized analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Infrastructure providers (database, hosting) under strict data processing agreements.
  • Error tracking services (Sentry) to diagnose bugs — these receive anonymized crash reports.

Guest Use

You may use the app without creating an account. In this case swipe history and preferences are stored only on your device and are not sent to our servers.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@your-app.com.

Children

This app is intended for adults (parents and expecting parents). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. We'll notify you via email or in-app notice for material changes.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@your-app.com.
